The GPR Journal
Gratitude • Prayer • Reflection
This is a simple journal designed to help you connect with your faith on a daily basis.

Each night before midnight, take a few minutes to answer three prompts. Keep your answers brief; one sentence per prompt is enough.

• Today, I am thankful for...
• Today, I would like to pray (for)...
• Today, I noticed God in my life when...

Try to do this every day for one month, but try not to give the same answer multiple times. At the end of the month, you'll be able to see all of your responses for that month at once--a collection of your gratitude, prayers, and reflections.
